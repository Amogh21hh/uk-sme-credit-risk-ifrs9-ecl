Scenario Sensitivity – Unweighted ECL (GBP m)

Macro overlay applies PD and LGD multipliers under three forward-looking scenarios. Final ECL is the probability-weighted average – this is the IFRS 9 reporting figure.

Methodology Summary

PD model: Logistic regression on WoE-transformed features (leverage, interest coverage, bureau score, sector, DPD, secured flag, years trading). Model selected via Information Value screening – leverage and interest coverage carry the strongest predictive power, consistent with PRA expectations for SME models.

IFRS 9 staging: Stage 1 = performing (12-month ECL); Stage 2 = SICR triggered (lifetime ECL); Stage 3 = credit-impaired (lifetime ECL). SICR proxies: PD > 15% or DPD ≥ 30.

Forward-looking overlay: ECL = Σ wᵢ · PDᵢ · LGDᵢ · EAD across Upside / Base / Downside scenarios. Scenario weights and multipliers are user-editable in the Excel model.

Validation: AUC, Gini, KS on a stratified hold-out; PSI to confirm population stability between train and test.
